Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

{# FAQ Item 1 #}
How do I launch a tool?

To launch a tool, simply navigate to the tool's detail page or find it in your "My Tools" or "Browse Tools" section and click the "Launch" button. This will open the tool in a new tab or redirect you to its interface.

{# FAQ Item 2 #}
How can I add a tool to my favorites?

You can add a tool to your favorites by clicking the heart icon typically found on the tool's card or its detail page. Your favorited tools will then appear in the "Favorites" section of your dashboard.

{# FAQ Item 3 #}
What if I encounter an issue with a tool?

If you experience problems with a specific tool, please check if the tool has its own support or documentation link on its detail page. For platform-related issues or if you can't find help, please contact our support team using the form below.
